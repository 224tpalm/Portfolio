    • Tyler Palm

    My name is Tyler Palm. I am a sophomore, attending FTHS Highchool, in Freehold, NJ. Growing up, I have always been interested in learning more, creating innovations, and had a love for creativity. Whether it was writing, drawing, or other activities, I enjoyed making things my own, making my creations unique. As a result, when Web Design was available in high school, I took the opportunity right away. I saw it as an opportunity to work with technology in a fun, different environment, that I was not used to. Today, I love the course, and pursue Web Design level one. Along with Web Design, I am in all honors classes, as I like to challenge myself educationally, and am a member of the FTHS Varsity Ice Hockey Team.
